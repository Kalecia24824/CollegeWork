Apples

by Kalecia McNeal

In this short article, we will learn about apples. From what they are to understanding how important apples are in everyday life!


What are Apples?

Now I know this question is way too simple because everyone knows what an apple is. It's a shiny red piece of fruit. But some apples like Granny Smith and Pink Lady apples can be green and pink respectively. As for the taste of the apple can range from sweet to tart.


Apples in Literature

For thousands of years, the apple has been used as a figure of symbolism from books, art, and to the big screen. Apples can be represented in many ways. Some meanings of apples can include love, knowledge and immortality. These meanings come from various mythologies including Greek, Norse and Christian stories.

How Healthy are Apples?

Finally, we will discuss how healthy apples are.

DISCLAIMER: Now I am not a health professional nor a doctor so it is important to you to talk to your doctor for more information.

With that out of the way, let's get into some possible benefits of eating apples can include:

  • Improving Heart Health
  • Antioxidants
  • Lowering Cholesterol
  • Reducing Blood Pressure
  • Reducing Inflammation
